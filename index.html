Disha Scholarship Cum Admission Test (D-SAT)
Disha Scholarship Aptitude Test (D-SAT) is a scholarship Exam for those students who are studying in std. X and aim to become an Engineer or Doctor. It gives students an opportunity to win scholarship up to 100% for XI & XII Integrated Classroom Program for Engineering and Medical Entrance Exams preparation. From the scholarship test, we will select 30 students for JEE and 30 students for NEET. These selected students will receive two years of free coaching from Disha Academy to prepare for their respective examinations. This initiative will significantly aid deserving students by providing them with the necessary resources and guidance to achieve their academic goals.
